Process

From call to clear
in four steps.

No guesswork. No upsells. A clear, documented process every time.

01
Free Inspection
A licensed technician visits your property, identifies the pest species, assesses the severity, and locates entry points. No charge, no obligation.
02
Custom Treatment Plan
We prepare a written treatment plan specific to your property, pest type, and risk level. You approve it before we start — no surprises on billing.
03
Treatment & Exclusion
We apply targeted, eco-safe treatments and seal structural entry points. Most residential jobs are completed in a single visit, with no displacement required.
04
30-Day Follow-Up
We return within 30 days to verify the treatment held. If activity is detected, we re-treat at no extra cost. You receive a clearance report for your records.

About Heer

Local knowledge.
Proven methods.

Heer Maintenance has been operating in Greater Vancouver since 2012. We understand how BC's wet climate, older housing stock, and dense urban areas create specific pest challenges that out-of-province operators often miss.

IPM Certified Technicians

All Heer technicians hold Integrated Pest Management certification and complete annual training on BC-specific pest species and regulations.

Eco-Safe Product Selection

We use Health Canada-registered pesticides with low mammalian toxicity and fast environmental breakdown. Safe for children, pets, and pollinators when applied correctly.

Full Reporting & Documentation

Every visit is logged and emailed to you — useful for strata requirements, property sales, and restaurant health inspections.
